About Us

Welcome to Balmeeki

Situated in Pathari-Morang, Established as Balmeeki Memorial Secondary Boarding School in 2054 is an educational institution affiliated with the National Examination Board (NEB). Our school provides comprehensive educational programs, ranging from primary level to the esteemed plus two [10+2] curriculum, ensuring a strong foundation and holistic development for our students.

In addition to our regular academic programs, we also offer tuition classes to support and enhance the learning experience of our students, ensuring they receive personalized attention and excel in their studies. Furthermore, we provide the opportunity for our students to earn international certificates, opening doors to global educational and career prospects, and empowering them to thrive in a competitive world.
